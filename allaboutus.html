WHAT WE DO:

Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, students are unable to attend classrooms as they did before
or one-on-one personal tutoring in their communities. Enter: Virtual Tutoring Academy
where students learn online, from the safety of their homes from experienced teachers at a very affordable price.
We offer online tutoring services for individual or small groups - an easy way to reenforce the educational needs
of young minds in the GTA area while observing physical/social distancing

WHO WE ARE:

We are a great group of passioned educators that got together and form Virtual Tutoring Academy.
We have a very practical process with techniquest that will bring clarity to complex topics,
with easy to remember clues and practice to bring theory into practical application in your child's life We deliver of this using the internet as we offer our tutoring servies online- bringing knowledfge to your house
We train the professionals of tomorrow!

SOCIAL RESPONSIBILITY:

Our Promise to our community is to be socially responsible by providing
the same levelof high quality tutoring to children of lower income
families through scholarships. There will be one free scholarship
available for every 10 paying students.
