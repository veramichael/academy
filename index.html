S.M.A.R.T. Goal #1

We will increase employee satisfaction by 5% by end of year 2020. Happy employees treat customers with respect and offer better customer service.

Customer Benefits

Customers will received better customer service.
Customers will received quicker response time and overall faster resolution times.

Processes

  • Job Description: We will ensure that an accurate and updated description of the roles, responsibilities and
    requirements for each type of position in the help desk is in place. Every employee will have a clear and well layout job description.
  • Training Plan: We will provide training plans that provide the required technical, business and customer service
    skill level to our customer service team to consistently meet our customer service level
  • Career development plans: We will create individual career development plans for every help desk member.
    These career development plans will be based on strategic goals for the help desk teams and the help desk analyst's career goals.
    These carrier development plans will be review yearly and will be integrated with compensation reviews, performance appraisals and promotions.
  • Personal performance appraisals: We will perfomed quarterly performance review appraisals to assess the
    individual goals, how the individual performance has contributed to team achievements and overall enterprise success.
  • Employee satisfaction: We will create the right processes and procedures to capture and measure employee
    satisfaction based on monthly employees surveys. Most imporantly this valuable input will be use to take action and
    manage employee satisfaction. Our ultimate goal is to have happy employees, this will in turn manifest in increase
    employee retention and productivity
  • Compensation: We will put together a competitive compensation program that will reflect competitive salary and
    benefit plans for each position.If necessary each position's compensation will be adjust to make sure salaries are
    competitive according to the market. We will perform yearly adjustment to employee's compensation based on employees
    performance appraisal's results.
  • Rewards and Recognition: We will put together a comprehensive rewards and recognition plan that will reward
    excelent performance of help desk analyst and teams. We will introduce the "employee of the month" award to reward the
    most outstanding analyst every month. We will introduce a "best team of the quarter" award that will provide an extra
    2 days of vacation for every employee member of the team that outperformed every other team in the quarter.

Measurements

  • Employee satisfaction feedback: Monthly survey processes will be use to capture and monitor employee satisfaction
    level. Action will be taken as well to improved identified areas as per employee feedback in a quarterly basis.
  • Employee turnover: We will measure employee turnover rates every quarter. Our goal is to have an overall turnover
    rate of 5% per year or less. we will measure position turnover rate due to promotion or transfers as well as negative turnover
    rates due to employees leaving the company. There will be an exit interview conducted with every employee that leaves the
    company to capture reasons for leaving and use this information to improve retention.
  • Unplanned absence days per employee: We have set internally a maximum number of unplanned absence days to 10 days
    per year per employee. We will track and measure unplanned employee absences monthly and engage employees during our quarterly
    employee performance reviews. the goal is to create dialog with employee and to seek for ways to decrease number of unplanned
    absence days cooperatively.

S.M.A.R.T. Goal #2

To improve our customer service levels by reducing our response time to all our client's request by 15% by the end of year 2021.

Customer Benefits

We are looking to increase customer productivity by quickly addressing their needs.

Processes

  • Financial Resources: Assign 30% of our operational budget to higher 5 new customer service representatives, train them and onboard them.
  • Physical Environment: To provide our customer service team members with ergonomic equipment including: better headsets, ergonomic mouse
    and keyboard, bigger monitors, adjustable desk (that will allow them to work standing or sitting).
  • Infrastructure: We will enhance our process with a new ticketing system that will allow our customer service team to log in tickets faster,
    the new ticketing system will have our clients contact and technology pre-loaded.
  • Incoming Contact Distribution System: Introduce the usage of Microsoft Teams, this will allow our team members to quickly interact with
    each other and escalate to management, via chat, video conference or voice over IP phone; getting faster response time.
  • Remote Support Tools: We will install remote desk software to allow our customer service representative to log in to the customers'
    computer remotely and assess & diagnost the problem faster.
  • Knowledge Management: Our new ticketing system comes with a knowledge management module. This knowledge management module will allow our
    customer service team similar incidents this customer has reported before (access to history of incidents). This module also provides answers to
    Frequently Asked Questions.
  • Reporting Tools: Our customer service team will have the ability to run multiple reports about the customer that he/she is servicing as
    well as all the software and hardware the customer uses.

Measurements

  • Average time to respond: We will measure and evaluate periodically key events: customer calls, customer service answers, issue is logged,
    and ticket is created, issue is resolved, and ticket is closed.
  • Customer feedback: We will deploy a new system of customer surveys to capture customer feedback. We will associate feedback with customer,
    incident, and customer service representative, and evaluate accordingly.
  • Periodic Customer Satisfaction Management: Our management will meet with every one of our clients every 3 months for a quarterly review to
    assess customer satisfaction and capture feedback.

To improve customer satisfaction by 10% by end of year 2020.

Customer Benefits

We are looking to provide our customers with excellent customer service. We will enhance our processes and this will result in fast and effective issue resolution.
We will measure customer satisfaction with our monthly customer surveys.

Measurements

  • Customer satisfaction surveys: We will set up a weekly customer survey to capture customer satisfaction. We will evaluate every feedback provided.

S.M.A.R.T. Goal #4

To decrease the total incident resolution time by 10% by end of year 2020.

Customer Benefits

Our customers will benefits from a faster resolution to their technical problems. We are looking to increase our customer productivity by resolving technical issues faster and effectively.

Processes

  • Incident Resolution: We will ensure that our customers have received a satisfactory solution to their issue or a functional
    workaround has been found.
  • Proactive detection and remediation: We will implement daily advance monitoring and diagnostics (scheduled to run every night)
    to automatically detect issues, send notification to the right personal and trigger corrective action during nightly maintenance windows in
    order to have these issues resolved daily while the customers are off line.
  • Service Level Management: We will perform Service Level Managment reviews with every customer once a month in order to collect
    feedback and enhance agreed upon process between service desk and our customers. Our main goal is to achieve our Service Level Agreements
    and Operational Level Agreements goals and performed up to our customers expectations.
  • Change Management: We will performed monthly upgrades to user systems during the last weekend of the month(according to our change
    management processes). These upgrades will be completed without disruption to the users. The service desk team will have an input and authority
    to stop any change process if customer is not ready or change process was not followed accordingly.
  • Configuration Management: We will reviews our Configuration Management Process with our customers every month in order to update recent
    changes and keep reliable and current details on Client's support and overall IT infrastructure. Service Desk agents will know the most recent
    configuration of customer's equipment when they call.
  • Continuous Improvement: We will put in place monthly reviews and improvements processes workshops with our customers in order to
    understand and capture our customer's feedback.
  • Prioritization: We will put in place processes for prioritization of incidents and service requests. This will ensure effective
    allocation and utilization of support resources.

Measurements

  • Average time to respond: We will daily monitor, measure and report average response time from our service desk team to incoming
    customers' calls. We will use these results to follow up with help desk representative that are struggling and help them to improve with
    coaching. These reports will also be use to improve processes and software use to receive calls.
  • Abandon rate: We will daily monitor, measure and report on abandon rate numbers. These reports will help us identify patterns and
    when these users are not getting answer in a timely manner. Armed with this information we will follow up with the respective service desk
    representative that were on duty when these abandoned rate happen. We will increase number of representive and adjust oncall software as needed.
  • Incident resolution time: We will daily monitor, measure and report on the average time required by our support team to restore
    services and resolve incidents to our customer satisfacion. We will compare these numbers to our target resolution times and follow up with
    cases where our target times were not met by service desk representatives. Using these reports we will understand if we need to add more
    resources and enhance our processes.

S.M.A.R.T. Goal #5

To reduce the number of daily outstanding calls by 10% by the end of the year

Customer Benefits

Customers will benefit from improved customer service, increase in productivity and cost savings

Processes

  • Incident Status: Update the status of all incidents as these move towards resolution. Make this status available to
    customers and help desk management throughout the day, in order to monitor progress of any incidents at any time.
  • Ticket Monitoring: Daily conduct random inspection of ticket content to ensure every incident ticket is
    documented according to preset high quality standard. Perform weekly revision of every ticket for quality assurance purposes.
  • Prioritization of Incidents: Every incident ticket will be assigned a priority level as agreed with customers.
    Each priority level will have an expected resolution time assigned to it. We will performed daily inspections to ensure all
    incident ticket have the right priority assigned and that each one of these incidents has been resolved within the expected time.
  • Continuous Improvement: All processess and procedures will be reviewed every month during a monthly workshop exercise
    with customer, help desk team, management, and optimization team in order to find and implement improvements to existing processes.
  • Incident Closure: We will perform weekly audits to ensure all incidents were close only after the following is true:
    1)incident was resolved according to customer requirements 2)all related information to the incident was properly capture and
    customer is in agreement that incident can be closed and has signed accordingly.
  • Call Monitoring: Management, Supervisors, and Coaches will daily and ramdomly seat with help desk analysts to listen
    to incoming call from customers. This exercise will be use for coaching and mentoring purposes in order to increase efficiency.
  • Escalation of Incidents: We will implement an escalation process whereby any help desk analyst will have the ability
    to escalate immediately when facing a roadblock or a deadend that is preventing them to resolve an incident. These escalation will
    be measure and classify in order to understand the type of roadblocks and empower help desk analyst to overcome them.

Measurements

  • Incident resolution time: We will run weekly reports to measure how many incidents were resolved within expected
    resolution time according to existing SLAs and how many issues breach existing SLAs. As a team, we will look for ways to reduce
    the amount of incidents that took longer that expected to be resolved.
  • Backlog Management: We will run reports three times a day (morning, afternoon, and evenings)in order to monitors
    the number of open issues and how long have they been open for (age). This reports will be use to address issues open longer
    than expected and find ways to decrease resolution time going forward.
  • Hierarchical escalations: We will run daily reports to measure the number of times help desk analysts required to escalate
    to supervisor/manager in order to resolve an issues. This reports will also include the reason for the escalation.
