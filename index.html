Support Service
Operation Plan
2020-2021
By: Michael Vera

We are looking to streamline all our helpdesk processes for the 2020-2021 fiscal year following ITIL guideline.
Our Help Desk will deliver top value for our clients by re-aligning every IT resource to addres an specific business needs.
Our team of professional IT experts will be able to effectively provide support around the clock 365 days a year helping our customers thrive

Standards

Our Help Desk team believes in holding ourselves to the highest standards that is why we have implemented the following:

to excellence, efficiency, and service quality.
Second: Out team has enhanced our Help Desk operation, processes and procedures according to the ITIL guidelines.
We believe our customers deserve nothing but the best and most effective support, and we know our team will deliver that every time!

S.M.A.R.T. Goal #1

Increase employee satisfaction by 10% by end of year 2020.

Customer Benefits

We believe that happy employees, properly equiped and trained, will be motivated to take good care of our customers.

Processes

  • Orientation Training Plan: We will equip and position every new employee for success by providing a new onboarding and training plan.
    Our new team members will also receive a reference online manual, they will have access to knowledge base databases, and will receive a detail job description
    that will state clearly state their rights, duties, responsibilities and our expectation.
  • Career Development: Every member of the HelpDesk team will take 8 hours of monthly mandatory training; this training will lead to certifications.
    These certifications, along with high performance levels at work will be taken under consideration for promotion to higher positions and salary review processes.
  • Employee Satisfaction: Employees will regularly provide their inputs and share their level of satisfacion using surveys, dropping their inputs on opinion boxes,
    taking anonymous surveys on line, and by talking to their direct supervisors as part of our new open door policy. Management will take action on input provided
    in order to continously improve our working environment. We will build a better company together and we will listen to our employees' voices.
  • Ongoing Skill Assessment and Training Plans: Our HelpDesk team will have access to own demand online training 24/7 to be access at their convenience;
    The training available will help to enhance multiple areas: soft skills, technical skills, leadership skill, and many others. This training will lead to recognition,
    potential promotion and potential salary increase.
  • Compensation Program: Employees will be directly rewarded for their efforts helping us improve our customer service metrics and on their efforts
    helping us make a better work environment for all. A new compensation plan and additonal bonus will be directly connected with customer service
    improvements, valuable ideas and extraordinary efforts perform by our employees. This awards and incentives will be provided on a personal level and on a team level.
  • Service Improvement Plans: Starting immediately we will capture customer input using customer surveys. This input will be capture, monitor
    and evaluated in a regular basis to measure improvement levels.
  • Physical Environment and Ergonomics: We will replace current obsolete equipment with ergonomic and advanced equipment;
    in order to empower our employees with the right tools to deliver great customer service.

Measurements

  • Employee Satisfaction: Monthly survey processes will be use to capture and monitor employee satisfaction level. Action will be taken as
    well to improved identified areas as per employee feedback in a quarterly basis.
  • People Turnover ratios: We will measure employee retention rates and those numbers relate to employee satisfaction.
  • Measure Unplanned Absenteeism: We will track and measure unplanned employee absences and engage employees accordingly to understand
    relation with employee satisfaction rates and seek for ways to decrease number of days lost due to absenteeism by working closely with our employees.

S.M.A.R.T. Goal #2

To improve our customer service levels by reducing our response time to all our client's request by 15% by the end of year 2021.

Customer Benefits

We are looking to increase customer productivity by quickly addressing their needs.

Processes

  • Financial Resources: Assign 30% of our operational budget to higher 5 new customer service representatives, train them and onboard them.
  • Physical Environment: To provide our customer service team members with ergonomic equipment including: better headsets, ergonomic mouse
    and keyboard, bigger monitors, adjustable desk (that will allow them to work standing or sitting).
  • Infrastructure: We will enhance our process with a new ticketing system that will allow our customer service team to log in tickets faster,
    the new ticketing system will have our clients contact and technology pre-loaded.
  • Incoming Contact Distribution System: Introduce the usage of Microsoft Teams, this will allow our team members to quickly interact with
    each other and escalate to management, via chat, video conference or voice over IP phone; getting faster response time.
  • Remote Support Tools: We will install remote desk software to allow our customer service representative to log in to the customers'
    computer remotely and assess & diagnost the problem faster.
  • Knowledge Management: Our new ticketing system comes with a knowledge management module. This knowledge management module will allow our
    customer service team similar incidents this customer has reported before (access to history of incidents). This module also provides answers to
    Frequently Asked Questions.
  • Reporting Tools: Our customer service team will have the ability to run multiple reports about the customer that he/she is servicing as
    well as all the software and hardware the customer uses.

Measurements

  • Average time to respond: We will measure and evaluate periodically key events: customer calls, customer service answers, issue is logged,
    and ticket is created, issue is resolved, and ticket is closed.
  • Customer feedback: We will deploy a new system of customer surveys to capture customer feedback. We will associate feedback with customer,
    incident, and customer service representative, and evaluate accordingly.
  • Periodic Customer Satisfaction Management: Our management will meet with every one of our clients every 3 months for a quarterly review to
    assess customer satisfaction and capture feedback.

S.M.A.R.T. Goal #3

To improve customer satisfaction by 10% by end of year 2020.

Customer Benefits

We are looking to provide our customers with excellent customer service. We will enhance our processes and this will result in fast and effective issue resolution.
We will measure customer satisfaction with our monthly customer surveys.

Processes

  • Self-Help:We will provide our customers with the following: ability to open and track a service ticket online;
    the ability to access an FAQ online and access to knowledge base using IVR capabilities to quickly answers their concerns.
  • Customer satisfaction management: We will follow up immediately with unsatisfy customer and capture what went wrong in order to improve our operating procedures.
  • Service Requests: We will enhance the monitoring process of each request from inception (assigning the right resources during scheduling)
    until completion only closing once customer is satisfied. Capturing any incident that may have occurred during the execution of the service request
    (in order to improve in future occasions)
  • Incident/Service Request logging: We will optimize our current incident and service logging process in order to bring consistency in the way we log all request.
    These logs should be easily accessible and simple to retrieve if and when a customers follows up and requires information.
  • Feedback Management: We will capture, store, and measure customer satisfaction using multiple tools including the followings:
    incident resolution feedback, monthly customer satisfaction survery, quarterly management follow ups with our customers.
  • Incident Status: We will deployed a new list of status updates agreed upon with our customers and periodically
    send updates to our customer throughout the life cycle of an incident
  • Incident Closure We will enhance our processes of incident closure ensuring customers satisfacion and agreement before the incidents are closed

Measurements

  • Customer satisfaction surveys: We will set up a weekly customer survey to capture customer satisfaction. We will evaluate every feedback provided.
  • Customer feedback: We will aim to capture every feedback from our customer after every issue resolution, after every interaction with our customer,
    and during management meetings with our customer.
  • Periodic end-user satisfaction We wil put periodic customer satisfaction surveys designed to measure overall customer satisfaction with the support center level of service

S.M.A.R.T. Goal #4

To decrease the total incident resolution time by 10% by end of year 2020.

Customer Benefits

Our customers will benefits from a faster resolution to their technical problems. We are looking to increase our customer productivity by resolving technical issues faster and effectively.

Processes

  • Incident Resolution: We will ensure that our customers have received a satisfactory solution to their issue or a functional workaround has been found.
  • Proactive detection and remediation: We will implement advance monitoring and diagnostics to automatically detect issues, send notification to the right
    personal and ensure corrective action is taken to resolve issues immediately.
  • Service Level Management: We will implement and periodically enhance Service Level Managment process between service desk and our customers in order to
    meet existing Service Level Agreements and Operational Level Agreements to our customers satisfaction
  • Change Management: We will performed upgrades to user systems (according to our change management processes) that are made without disruption to the users.
    The service desk team will have an input and authority to stop any change process if customer is not ready or change process was not followed accordingly.
  • Configuration Management: We will work with our customers in order to implement a Configuration Management Process that will provide reliable and current details
    about support infrastructure and overall IT infrastructure. Service Desk agents will know the configuration of customer's equipment when they call.
  • Continuous Improvement: We will put in place continous review and improvements processes that will be take under consideration our customer's feedback.
  • Prioritization: We will put in place processes for prioritization of incidents and service requests. This will ensure effective allocation and utilization of support resources.

Measurements

  • Average time to respond: We will constantly monitor, measure and report average response time from our service desk team to incoming customers' calls.
  • Abandon rate: We will monitor, measure and report on abandon rate numbers; understanding the number of contact attempts from support channels
    that were ended by the user prior to establishing contact with support center resources.
  • Incident resolution time: We will monitor, measure and report on the average time required by our support team to restore services and resolve incidents to our customer satisfacion.
